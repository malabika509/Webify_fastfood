Mugcake veg


Ingredients:


  1. 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  2. 3 tbsp granulated sugar
  3. 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  4. 1/8 tsp salt
  5. 1/4 tsp baking powder
  6. 3 tbsp milk
  7. 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  8. 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:


In a microwave-safe mug, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder.
Add the milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the mug. Mix until the batter is smooth and well combined.
Microwave the mug on high for about 1 minute and 30 seconds to 2 minutes. The cooking time may vary depending on your microwave's wattage, so keep an eye on it. The cake should rise and firm up but still be slightly gooey in the center.
Carefully remove the mug from the microwave (it will be hot), and let it cool for a minute.
Optionally, top it with whipped cream, ice cream, chocolate chips, or your favorite toppings.
Grab a spoon and dig in!

Mushroom Sauce veg


Ingredients:


  1. 3 cloves of garlic chopped
  2. 1 medium-sized onion chopped
  3. 1 green chilli chopped
  4. 6 to 7 button mushrooms sliced
  5. 1 tbsp vegetable or olive oil
  6. 2 tbsp butter
  7. 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  8. 1 cup milk
  9. Salt, sugar and black pepper to taste
  10. Any type of cheese (optional but i recommend Mozarella)

Instructions:


Heat the pan and add the oil and butter.
Add the chopped onions and stir it till it translucent and then add the chopped garlic and then the mushrooms.
Add the all-purpose flour and stir it till it changes colour but not enough to burn it, then add the milk gradually as it thickens.
Add the chopped chillies, salt, sugar and pepper to taste.
You can add cheese at this point or you can take it off the heat as per your desired consistency. The more you cook it, the thicker it becomes!
Enjoy your delicious white mushroom sauce with a toast or as a side for pasta!

Crispy Potatoes veg


Ingredients:


  1. 3 potatoes cubed bite-sized
  2. 4 tbsp vegetable or olive oil
  3. 2 cloves of chopped garlic
  4. 1 green chilli chopped
  5. 1 tsp of chilli powder, salt, sugar,
    italian seasoning and pepper
  6. 1 tbsp lemon juice
  7. Any chopped herb for topping
    8. This is one of the easiest snack you can make!
    Its crispy, tangy, spicy and will satisfy you salty cravings fosho!

Instructions:


In a pan, heat the oil. Once it's hot, add the peeled, chopped and thoroughly washed potatoes to the pan.
Add the chopped garlic and chillies to the oil along with the potatoes.
After 2minutes of stirring and frying once all the potatoes are coated with oil, add the salt.
Cover and leave the flame on medium and occassionally stir the potatoes.
Fry them till they are golden and cooked through, then add all the spices and seasonings.
Top it off with the lemon juice, give it a last stir and garnish with your chopped herb of choice.
Enjoy!!

Egg Fried Rice


Ingredients:


  1. Bowl of a day old rice (the dish comes out best if it is dry, basically)
  2. 2 tbsp of chopped garlic and ginger
  3. 1-2 eggs
  4. 1 chopped green chilli
  5. 1 tsp salt, 2tsp of pepper, 1sp of red chilli powder
  6. 1 tbsp of vinegar (Apple Cider Vinegar(ACV) is a healthy choice!)
  7. 1/3 cup of chopped green onions

Instructions:


Heat your pan on high till it smokes, add oil and a pinch of salt to it.
Add your cup of rice and stir till it is well coated and fry it for a minute and then add the chopped garlic and ginger to it.
Now make space in the middle of the pan for the egg, 1 tbsp of oil and crack the egg directly into the middle and scramble the eggs
Mix it with the rice and add salt, pepper and red chilli powder as per your taste.
Add your chopped green onions and sprinkle the vinegar and stir till the rice is fried well.
Ta-da! Your steaming bowl of egg fried rice is readyyy to be savoured!

Pancakes veg


Ingredients:


  1. 2 cups of all-purpose flour(makes around 3 pancakes!)
  2. 1 tsp of baking powder
  3. 2 tbsp of melted butter
  4. 4 tbsp of powdered sugar (as per your need)
  5. 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  6. 4 tbsp milk
  7. Chopped up fruits or chocolate (optional)
  8. Maple syrup, fresh fruit, or any toppings of you like. Be Creative!

Instructions:


Mix all the dry ingredients first in a bowl.
Add the melted butter and gradually mix the milk till you get a batter(not too thin, not too thick!).
Add your chopped fruits or chocolate if you want.
Then heat the pan and add 1 tbsp butter, once its hot, add a big spoonful of batter. Let it cook on low till it is golden on both sides.
Make all the pancakes with batter and then add your toppings of choice! My favourite is the nutella and strawberry combo!
Now go ahead and dig in while its warm!
